What is Neuroplasticity?

Neuroplasticity is the concept that our minds and our intelligence are not fixed, and that they can grown and develop through mindful excercise or practise.

How might one engage with the principles of neuroplasticity?

The concept of 'neurons that fire together, wire together', means that the more we use certain pathways in the brain, the more they are strengthened and developed.

Techniques like practising mindfulness, and learning new things can develop our brains further. Someone may do more puzzles, read books, learn a new skill or game, or play a lot of chess to develop their Neuroplasticity.

As mentioned above, doing conscious excercises that challenge the brain can help to develop neuroplasticity. Research Growth Mindset Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

Growth Mindset is a way of viewing challenges and setbacks. People with a Growth Mindset see challenges as things that can be overcome through hard work and effort. In contrast, a Fixed Mindset would believe that one has the ability or not to overcome a challenge, regardless of effort. A Fixed Mindset person may then not bother to make an effort, believing that their effort will have no effect on the outcome.
